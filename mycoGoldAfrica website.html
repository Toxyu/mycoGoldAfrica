About MycoGold Africa

Mission Statement

To empower youth through sustainable agriculture.

Vision Statement

A hunger-free, mentally healthy, and climate-conscious Africa.

Why We Exist

In a time where youth unemployment is soaring and food insecurity is a growing concern, MycoGold Africa offers a holistic solution.

What We Do

We train youth in sustainable agriculture and support their mental health.

Why it matters: Too many young Africans wake up each day with nothing to do.

Our Impact Areas

Empowering youth with skills and opportunities to escape poverty.

SDG 2: Zero Hunger

Promoting sustainable agriculture to ensure food security.

SDG 3: Good Health

Supporting mental health to foster well-being among youth.
